Mysticism & Mathematics: The Lo Shu Algorithm (洛書)

The Lo Shu is more than a puzzle; it is a cosmogonical map describing the perfect order of the moving universe, the foundation of Feng Shui (風水) and Eastern numerology.

1. Etymology and Origin

The name stems from Luo (洛), the sacred tributary of the Yellow River, and Shu (书), meaning 'Book', 'Script', or 'Document'. It represents the sacred knowledge delivered by nature to mankind to restore balance (Qi) to the world.

2. The Magic Constant M

In a magic square of order n, the sum of every row, column, and diagonal is uniquely determined by the formula:

M =
n (n² + 1) 2

For the Lo Shu (order n=3), the calculation reveals the number of harmony:
M = (3 × (9 + 1)) / 2 = 15.

Interestingly, 15 is the number of days in each of the 24 solar terms of the Chinese calendar.

3. Combinatorial Analysis: Chaos vs. Order

Without the wisdom of the Wu Xing (五行), you would face an army of possibilities. Total permutations of numbers 1 to 9 are given by 9 Factorial (9!):

9! = 9 × 8 × ... × 1 = 362,880

Out of these thousands of combinations, only 8 are harmonic. The probability of solving the Lo Shu by pure chance is 0.0022%.

4. Geometry & Symmetry (Dihedral Group D₄)

The 8 solutions are not isolated: they are mirrors of one another. Once a "seed" solution is found, the other 7 are obtained through:

  • Rotations: 90°, 180°, and 270°.
  • Reflections: Mirroring the square horizontally, vertically, or along the diagonals.

5. The 10x10 Abyss: Combinatorial Explosion

While order 3 is within human reach, order 10 enters the realm of computational impossibility. Total combinations are 100!:

100! ≈ 9.33 × 10¹⁵⁷

Even using the world's most powerful supercomputer, the time for a full scan would exceed 10¹³⁰ years. This figure dwarfs the Age of the Universe (1.38 × 10¹⁰ years), making the 10x10 square an eternal enigma.
